CollectionSupplementaryViewFactoryType

public protocol CollectionSupplementaryViewFactoryType

An instance conforming to CollectionSupplementaryViewFactoryType is responsible for initializing and configuring collection view supplementary views to be consumed by an instance of CollectionViewDataSourceProvider.
